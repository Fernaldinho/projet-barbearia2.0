# .stitch/DESIGN.md - Modern Heirloom Design System

## 🌑 THE SOUL OF THE SITE
- **Aura:** Modern Heirloom / Precision Noir
- **Mood:** Luxury Barber, Traditional yet high-tech, Premium craftsmanship.
- **Goal:** Differentiate from generic SaaS with timeless, high-contrast layouts.

## 🎨 PALETTE: THE NOIR GOLD
- **Primary (Gold):** `#ffe2ab` (Amber), `#ffbf00` (Deep Gold), `#ffe088` (Highlight)
- **Background:** `#131313` (Deep Matte Charcoal)
- **Surface:** `#1c1b1b` (Low Elevation), `#2a2a2a` (High Elevation)
- **On-Surface:** `#e5e2e1` (Off-white, 90% contrast)
- **Accent:** `#e9c349` (Secondary Gold)
- **Safe Ban:** No Violet, No Indigo, No Teal as primary.

## 📐 TOPOLOGY: THE PRECISION CUT
- **Type:** Sharp, Geometric, Technical.
- **Roundness:** 2px - 4px (Minimalism).
- **Layout:** Asymmetric layers, overlapping depth, massive typographic hero.
- **Glassmorphism:** Solid edges (1px), 12px blur, 40% opacity surfaces.

## 🖋️ TYPOGRAPHY: THE EDITORIAL
- **Headline:** `Noto Serif` (Italic variations for emphasis)
- **Body/System:** `Inter` (High-readability, narrow tracking)

## ✨ MOTION: THE FLUID PRECISION
- **Ease:** Staggered reveals, spring-physics transforms.
- **Micro:** Subtle scale (95%) on CTAs, hover glows.

## 🤖 DESIGN SYSTEM NOTES FOR STITCH GENERATION

[PRECISION NOIR DESIGN SYSTEM]
